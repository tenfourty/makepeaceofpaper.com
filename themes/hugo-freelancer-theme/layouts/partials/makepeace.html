#MAKEPEACEOFPAPER is a movement to put peace first- peace with one another, between nations, between political groups. Listen more. Talk less. Be willing to risk giving up more of our position to secure peace.

How? We take the bad news we find in the papers or online and turn it into something beautiful- an origami peace crane. Then we take this beauty and place it for the world to see or by posting it to the people of power, influence. All as an invitation to seek peace.

Interested in joining us? It's simple:

  1. Check out the rest of this website and our video
  2. Find a story and make it beautiful. (Look here for how to make an origami crane) Click here to learn how – it will take you 5 minutes.
  3. Display your Peace of Paper crane or mail to to one of your elected representatives.
  4. Make a stand and share a picture of your crane online with the hashtag #makepeaceofpaper

It’s something beautiful, something good. It is the statement that the best victory we can imagine is peace. Make Peace of Paper. #makepeaceofpaper
